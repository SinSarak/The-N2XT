About Story

Throughout my career, which has included roles at both local and multinational companies, I have consistently encountered challenges in my work. I have often challenged my line managers and subordinates about the need for better tools to improve our workflow.

However, when it comes time to actually deploy these tools, the cost and time required for implementation often proves to be the biggest hurdle, and we end up not implementing the project.

The world has evolve alot recently with the new revolution of technology like low code/ no code movement and AI, therefore I truely believe it’s time now for the distrupter to join the app development spaces and I’ve been prepared since 2019 and testing it with my work and clients.

The world has changed significantly in recent years due to new technological advancements like low-code/no-code and AI. As a result, I believe the time has come for a disruptive force to enter the app development industry. I have been preparing for this since 2019 by testing my ideas with my work and clients.

The future of small businesses is bright. Affordable cloud-based software, AI and data are leveling the playing field and providing small businesses with the tools they need to compete with large corporations.
